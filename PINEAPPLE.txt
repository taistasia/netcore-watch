NON-NEGOTIABLE ENGINEERING RULES

Dirty rectangle redraw only during runtime ticks.

Strategy:

Border drawn each frame (small cost acceptable).

Previous fill width cached.

Q16 progress converted using:

(int64 innerW * qProgress) >> 16

First draw clears interior.

Grow → fill delta only.

Shrink → clear delta only.

No fillScreen during tick.

No residual pixels.
